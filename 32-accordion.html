FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know. Can't find the answer? Contact us.

Pricing & Plans
01
Is there a free plan available?
▾
Yes! Our Free tier gives you up to 3 projects, 5 GB of storage, and access to all core features — no credit card required. You can upgrade at any time as your needs grow.
02
Can I change my plan at any time?
▾
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan from the Settings → Billing page. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades are applied at the end of your current billing cycle. Unused credits are prorated automatically.
03
What payment methods do you accept?
▾
We accept all major credit cards and debit cards:
  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express
  • UPI & Net Banking (India)
  • PayPal
  • Wire transfer for annual enterprise contracts
Technical
04
What tech stack does your platform use?
▾
Our platform is built on a modern stack including React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL on the backend. We use edge CDN distribution globally and support webhooks, REST APIs, and GraphQL endpoints.
05
Is my data secure and GDPR compliant?
▾
Security is our top priority. We are SOC 2 Type II and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We offer data residency options in EU and US regions.
06
Do you offer an API or SDK?
▾
Yes — we provide a REST API and official SDKs for JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, and Go. Our API uses standard OAuth 2.0 for authentication and returns JSON responses. Rate limits vary by plan.
Support
07
What kind of support do you offer?
▾
Free users get community forum support. Pro users get email support with a 24h SLA. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated Slack channel, SLA of 4h, and a named customer success manager.
